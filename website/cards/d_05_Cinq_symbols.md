# 5 d'Epees — Symboles (Rider-Waite)

**Source :** Roebuck-Jones and Associates — connectwithyourdeck.com

## Symboles de la carte
### Main Figure
The figure looks back at the others. Arrogance and meanness or self- satisfaction at calling a well needed boundary that others have chosen not to accept.

### Red/Green
Vêtements The main figure is wearing red and green, passion and fertility, recognising this person's will and potential for growth through taking a stand.

### Deux figures
Giving up as they have left their swords behind, or abandonment where they have disagreed so strongly they have dropped their swords and walked away.

### Eaus Edge
Edge of the water is straight boundary drawn or a moment in time where there is no compromise.The water - represents emotion in the situation.

### Nuages And Sky
Grey clouds which appear to be breaking up and disappearing - it is as if the events in the card although difficult have shifted.  Breaking clouds revealing a clear blue sky - symbolic of clarity of thoughts.
